Electrical Engineer (Battery Specialist)

Palo Alto, CA - Engineering - Full-Time

Opener is a small company innovating at the leading edge of UAV technology, poised to transform the industry. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are actively adding to our team of engineers. We value bright individuals with a strong DIY, hands-on attitude, who work hard and thrive in a fast-paced environment. Join us and influence the future of aviation.

We are seeking a motivated individual to take on the role of battery specialist within our Electrical Engineering group.

Role Responsibilities

  • Develop an offline state-space lithium-based battery estimation model such as in Matlab/Simulink.
  • Devise and carry out lab experiments to test, tune and refine the model under the full range of operating temperatures, currents, depth of discharge, and cycle count.
  • Implement the model on an embedded MCU which accurately estimates SOC, SOE, and SOP in real-time over the full range of operating conditions for the service life of the battery.
  • Develop a battery characterization test to QC incoming batteries and test new samples.
  • Develop methods to perform a rapid pre-flight check of battery metrics.
  • Define safe operational minimums and maximums for the battery based on experimental results.
  • Depending on experience, assist with charger development and take ownership of BMS.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Undergraduate or postgraduate degree in Electrical Engineering.
  • Proficient at writing firmware (C/C++) for MCUs in embedded systems.
  • Experience with extended Kalman filter algorithms.
  • Familiarity with battery rapid-test methods such as dynamic response, impedance spectroscopy.
  • Hands-on electronics experience through student teams, co-op, and/or personal projects

Optional Qualifications

  • Circuit design, PCB layout, power electronics, SPI, UART, CAN.
  • Matlab/Simulink and Python experience.
  • Interest in RC planes, quadcopters, or aviation.

Personal Attributes

  • Hard-working, detail-oriented, resourceful, curious and enjoys being challenged
  • Always thinking about how to make things more efficient
  • Thrive in a fast-moving, stimulating, and supportive team environment
