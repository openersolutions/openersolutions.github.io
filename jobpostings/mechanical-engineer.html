Opener

Mechanical Engineer

Palo Alto, CA - Engineering - Full-Time

Opener is a small company innovating at the leading edge of UAV technology, poised to transform the industry. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are actively adding to our team of engineers. We value bright individuals with a strong DIY, hands-on attitude, who work hard and thrive in a fast-paced environment. Join us and influence the future of aviation.

Role Responsibilities

  • Develop automated manufacturing methods
  • CAD design of test equipment, jigs, and fixtures
  • Assist with brushless DC motor design
  • Integration of aircraft electrical and mechanical systems
  • Thermal and mechanical modeling and testing of electric propulsion systems
  • Assist in UAV flight testing both locally and at remote sites (some travel required)

Minimum Qualifications

  • Undergraduate or post-graduate degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering
  • Keen eye for detail, experience with quality control and manufacturing processes
  • Proficient with solid-modeling CAD software (preferably CATIA) and 2D documentation
  • Demonstrated hands-on experience through student teams, co-op, and/or personal projects
  • Experience using rigorous scientific methods for testing and troubleshooting

Preferred Qualifications

  • Mechatronics-related experience with machine design, testing, and prototyping
  • Interest in RC planes, quadcopters, and/or kit plane construction
  • Knowledge and experience with manufacturing processes: motor winding, CNC machining, carbon fiber composite layups

Personal Attributes

  • Resourceful, curious, never satisfied with the status quo
  • Always thinking about how to make things more efficient
  • Thrives in a fast-moving, stimulating, and supportive team environment
  • Strong work ethic and detail-oriented
  • Love for learning and being challenged

Applicants must be eligible to work in the United States and willing to relocate. To apply, please send your cover letter, resume, and unofficial transcripts to jobs@openersolutions.com. Please include “Mechanical Engineer” in the subject line.
