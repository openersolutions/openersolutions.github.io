Opener

UX/UI Software Engineer

Palo Alto, CA - Engineering - Full-Time

Opener is a small company innovating at the leading edge of UAV technology, poised to transform the industry. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are actively adding to our team of engineers. We value bright individuals with a strong DIY, hands-on attitude, who work hard and thrive in a fast-paced environment. Join us and influence the future of aviation.

We have an opening for a Software Engineer with 1 to 5 years of professional experience. New grads with coop work experience are welcome to apply. We are working on developing safe and reliable avionics for an unconventional UAV. The role will largely focus on developing a user friendly system for human control. The ideal candidate would be capable of working in other software domains as necessary.

Responsibilities and Duties

  • Develop software/firmware for projects related to user input and feedback systems
  • Contribute to the design of an intuitive human machine interface
  • Assist in UAV flight testing both locally and at remote sites (some travel required)

Minimum Qualifications

  • Undergraduate or postgraduate degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related
  • Strong software skills in C/C++ and a higher level language (Python, C#, or Java)
  • Hands-on experience through employment, student teams, co-op, and/or personal projects
  • Experience with Linux development
  • Experience with popular UI frameworks and design patterns

Optional Qualifications

  • Embedded development experience
  • Familiarity with fullstack development for web applications
  • Network programming experience
  • Interest in RC planes, quadcopters, or aviation

Personal Attributes

  • Resourceful, curious, and never satisfied with the status quo
  • Always thinking about how to make things more efficient
  • Strong work ethic and detail-oriented
  • Love learning and being challenged
