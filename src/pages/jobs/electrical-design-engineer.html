Opener is a small company innovating at the leading edge of UAV technology, poised to transform the industry. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are actively adding to our team of engineers. We value bright individuals with a strong DIY, hands-on attitude, who work hard and thrive in a fast-paced environment. Join us and influence the future of aviation.

Role Responsibilities

  • Develop hardware and firmware for projects related to power, sensor and UAV control systems
  • Test, characterize and iterate existing designs
  • Evaluate new sensor technologies and assess for applicability to the project
  • Assist in UAV flight testing both locally and at remote sites (some travel required)
  • Analyze UAV electronics and components for operational failures
  • Create production testing solutions for electronic assemblies

Minimum Qualifications

  • Undergraduate or post-graduate degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering
  • Strong understanding of analog and digital circuit design concepts
  • Proficient at PCB layout, electronic assembly, and troubleshooting circuits with an oscilloscope
  • Proficient at writing firmware (C/C++) for MCUs in embedded systems
  • Experience with common communication protocols (SPI, UART, CAN)
  • Hands-on experience through student teams, co-op, and/or personal projects

Preferred Qualifications

  • Practical understanding of switching power supply design and layout considerations
  • Familiarity with brushless DC motor control, lithium-based battery integration
  • Familiarity GPS and UAV sensors
  • Interest in RC planes, quadcopters, and/or kit plane construction
  • Programming experience in Matlab and/or Python

Personal Attributes

  • Resourceful, curious, and never satisfied with the status quo
  • Always thinking about how to make things more efficient
  • Thrive in a fast-moving, stimulating, and supportive team environment
  • Strong work ethic and detail-oriented
  • Love learning and being challenged
